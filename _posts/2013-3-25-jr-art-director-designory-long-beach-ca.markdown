##Job Summary: 

The Jr. Art Director is passionate about great design and will assist the Art Directors in concepting and layout for web, mobile and print pieces.

##Responsibilities:

* Apply design (GUI) to wireframes and/or concept sketches
* Collaborate with Project Management, Production, Art Buying and Studio to achieve the best solutions for assignments
* Produces story boards for client presentations on assigned projects
* May assist in presenting final layouts to client for approval
* Keeps current on design/production trends, materials and developments 
	
##Qualifications:

* 0-1 year related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. (Strong portfolio can replace any formal education requirements).
* Expert skills in Adobe Creative Suite (especially Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign)
* Print collateral design and typography experience a plus
* Passion for interactive design, trends in mobile, social media, and print design
* Interest or experience in automotive a plus
